Henri Matisse: The Cut-outs

A retrospective of Matisse's Cut-out period
Printed matter

+ More Info

At the end of his life, Henri Matisse turned almost exclusively to cut-out paper as his medium. This publication is a retrospective of this period of his life, explored using technical and conceptual ideas inspired by Matisse’s cut-outs.
